Our Team

Meet the people who power Spark — their photos and short notes about why they joined are below.

Founders

Savitur Ramasubramanian

Founder

I founded SPARK to give students like me a platform to share knowledge, inspire curiosity, and make STEAM education accessible worldwide. By connecting youth voices across countries, I wanted to create a space where learning becomes enjoyable, creative, and impactful.

Shriman Srinivas

Co-Founder

Hello! My name is Shriman Srinivas, and I joined this initiative because not only do I love making, editing, and recording videos, but I also love educating and helping people for a good cause—and that is exactly what SPARK is: a good cause! And I also love science, technology, engineering, and math; and by being a co-owner, I can not only make videos, but I can also foster leadership skills, helpfulness, STEAM skills, and a lot more! I hope to help a countless number of people to the best of my ability, and educate people whilst keeping my passion for making videos!

Yana Dineva

Member

I am passionate about STEAM and love sharing my knowledge with others. Joining SPARK allows me to channel this passion by inspiring kids worldwide. Originally from a small country in Bulgaria, I bring my unique background and enthusiasm to every learning experience.

Marcus Law

Member

Hello, I'm Marcus Law. I joined the SPARK program to educated other young people about STEAM subjects. I am primarily a science and math guy, and I feel other young people should be open to these subjects too. I want to make learning not a goal, but a journey.
