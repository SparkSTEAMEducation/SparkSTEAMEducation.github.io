Founders

Savitur Ramasubramanian

Founder

I founded SPARK to give students like me a platform to share knowledge, inspire curiosity, and make STEAM education accessible worldwide. By connecting youth voices across countries, I wanted to create a space where learning becomes enjoyable, creative, and impactful.

Shriman Srinivas

Co-Founder

Hi, I’m Shriman Srinivas. Along with Savithur, we started SPARK because we are passionate about making, editing, and recording videos, while also helping others through education for a good cause. I love STEAM (science, tech, engineering, art, math), and as a co-owner, I get to combine my passion for video creation with fostering leadership, teamwork, and critical STEAM skills. My goal is to educate and inspire as many people as possible while doing what I love.
